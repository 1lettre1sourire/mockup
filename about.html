Our Mission

Who we Are

1Letter1Smile is a nonprofit organization dedicated to reaching isolated elderly people through love and letter-writing. Welcoming people of all ages and cultures to write letters to the elderly to promote intergenerational support. We believe that letter-writers are the world savers and we are building the medium for everyone to do so.

Our Values

Three Principles

Simplicity

Informal and down-to-earth, each step in our process must be a simple & pleasant experience for everyone.

Flexibility

Changing to meet needs,
our success – and much of the fun – is in finding new ways to spread joy.

Love

Putting heart in everything,
we're stronger trusting each other, pulling in the same direction, and having fun.
