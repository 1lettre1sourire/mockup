Process

How It Works

Create

Brainstorm a happy memory or words of encouragement you'd like to share. Then, send your letter (at least 250 words) on our website.

Proofread

Your letter is read by one of our volunteers and then formatted to look like a real letter! It's ready to be sent to the care homes you selected through email.

Delivery

Care homes download, print, and distribute your letter to their elderly residents. Your recipient may respond through the contact information you provided!
